I am a Professor at the University of Sydney. My research is in radio transients, and I co-lead the transient projects (VAST) on the Australian SKA Pathfinder.

I teach computational science and data science for physics and astronomy students, and I'm particularly interested in online teaching and teaching innovation.

You can find me in Room 358 in the School of Physics, but email is the best way of getting in touch.
