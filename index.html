I am Head of School of Physics at the University of Sydney and a Chief Investigator in the OzGrav Centre of Excellence. My research is in radio transients, and I co-lead the Variables and Slow Transients (VAST) project on the Australian SKA Pathfinder.

I teach computational science and data science for physics and astronomy students, and I'm particularly interested in online teaching and teaching innovation.

You can find me in Room 214 in the School of Physics, but email is the best way of getting in touch.
